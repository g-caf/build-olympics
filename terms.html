Participants must be at least 18 years old and legally eligible to work in their country of residence. Employees of Amp Arena organizers and their immediate families are not eligible to participate.

  • Must be 18+ years of age
  • Must have legal work authorization
  • Cannot be an employee or family member of organizers
  • Must complete all registration requirements

3. Competition Structure

The competition consists of:

  • Four online qualifying rounds
  • Four finalists selected each week
  • Live final competition in San Francisco on October 29th, 2025
